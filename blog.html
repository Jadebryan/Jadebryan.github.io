Insights

Computing encompasses the study and use of computers, software, and systems to process and manage information.

The field of computing offers diverse career paths, including software development, data science, cybersecurity,network administration, and more.

The information processing cycle involves input, processing, output, and storageof data.Input is recieved, processed by the computer,and results in output, with storage ensuring data persistance for future use.

Computer systems comprise hardware and software components working together.Hardware includes the physical components like the processor and memory, while software encompasses the programs that enable the computer to perform tasks.

Computer security is vital to protect systems and data from unauthorized acces or damage. Concepts include encryption, firewalls, antivirus software, and user authentication to safeguard against cyber threats.

HTML (Hypertex Markup Language) and CSS(Cascading Style Sheet) are fundamental web development languages. HTML structures content on the web, while CSS styles and formats it.
